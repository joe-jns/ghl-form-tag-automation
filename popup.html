Auto-tag

GHL Form → Tag Automation

Open a form in the GoHighLevel builder. An "Auto-tag" button appears in the toolbar.

  1. Click Auto-tag.
  2. Pick (or create) a tag.
  3. The extension creates a folder + workflow + "Form submitted" trigger on this form + "Add tag" action, then publishes.

No backend, no data sent anywhere else: everything runs through your own GoHighLevel session.
